About I-LIT Tech

Learn about our mission, vision, and team

Our Mission

I-LIT Tech is dedicated to providing innovative data solutions and technology services to organizations and researchers. We specialize in data collection, statistical analysis, and technology implementation to support evidence-based decision making.

Our Expertise

With expertise in both biostatistics and technology, we offer unique solutions that bridge the gap between data collection and analysis. Our team is proficient in various data collection tools including Kobo Collect, Redcap, CS Pro, and Google Forms.

Our Approach

We believe in collaborative partnerships with our clients, working closely to understand their unique needs and delivering customized solutions that drive impact and innovation.
